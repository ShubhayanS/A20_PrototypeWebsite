About Aaruush '20

Over the years SRMIST has built and maintained a reputation for its state of the art facilities, as well as the experience and ethics it imbibes in its students. This forms the basis for achieving success in today's world.

Aaruush, one of the largest technological events of the country has kept up its tradition of innovation and has broken many frontiers.It has a knack for bringing out the best in you.

This techno-management fest is a platform for more than 70,000 young minds from not only India's but also the world's top institutions to bring forth their innovative thoughts and ideas.

From paper presentations and guest lectures to debates and quizzes, Aaruush plays host to a plenitude of activities, giving you the opportunity to interact with the brightest minds in the country.
